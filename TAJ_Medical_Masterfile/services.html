Services

At T.A.J. Medical, we strive to provide a full range of medical services for our patients.

For those of you without medical insurance or a medical card, please note our fees are as follows:

SERVICES COST
GP Consultation €60
Practice Nurse Consultation €35
Blood test €90
Physiotherapy (First Visit) €60
Physiotherapy (Subsequent Visit) €40

Below are some of the services available in our Clinic. If you require medical care that is not listed below, please contact us for more information.

General GP Visits

Consultations are held in our clinic at the IFSC. In special circumstances, home visits can be arranged.

Family Planning and Contraception

All of our GPs are able to provide contraceptive advice. This will include advice on the various types of contraception available, and suitability.

Men considering contraception methods, may wish to discuss vasectomy.

Health Assessments

We offer a full range of assessments and same are provided on the principle that early detection of illness can mean easier management of the illness leading to a greater chance of recovery.

Maternity Services

Maternity care entitles you to 6 antenatal and 2 post natal visits free of charge.

Phlebotomy Services

We offer blood tests and phlebotomy services to patients suffering from haemochromatosis. Please contact us to arrange an appointment with our Practice Nurse.

Influenza Vaccinations

All patients over the age of 65 are advised to receive flu vaccination. Clinics start in Autumn and run for throughout the Winter. Special provisions will be implemented in due course for the distribution of the Covid-19 Phizer vaccine.
